In Grade 10 I survived the school year with having small talk with just anyone I could talk to. Morish was there consistently and basically allowed me to talk his ear off whenever I wanted. Sometimes I feel bad because in those 3 years of friendship with him, it's usually me talking his ear off and never him talking to me. Sometimes I wondered if I was ever bothering him but on the other hand, I'm glad that he's kind enough to just let me talk to himm about anything and everything really. He's been such a great friend and I'm so glad to have met him and gotten closer to him in my life here in pisay. I hope that even if college has different plans for us both, we'll still keep in touch and remain good friends.

The Star Arcana has to do ultimately with the freedom to be one's self. This card also represents our reason for being, our mission in this lifetime. If we let go of the idea that we are supposed to be in control, we can more easily notice and appreciate the synchronicities that are nudging us along.

Morish Navales

A charismatic young man who charms everyone with his smile as bright as a star. He is a kind soul who brings a positive influence in anyone's lives.
