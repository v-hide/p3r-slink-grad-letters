Hermit

8

We sat next to each other in Grade 10 and our friendship bloomed over small talk and being forced to do school work together whenever the task needed you to pair up with someone, usually your seatmate. He was also the only other person that ate as fast as I did for lunch and we were both the first from our batch to reach the canteen nearly everyday to the point where our classmates started betting on who would get food and manage to get back to the classroom first even if it was never a competition for us. He's a relatively quiet guy, often choosing to keep to himself but he always makes sure to talk to his close friends, especially the dormers. Having someone like him, who is honestly just there, sometimes kind of an airhead, sometimes kind of serious, a little bit of everything honestly, is reassuring. He's a nice guy with good intentions and I hope to still keep in touch even after graduating, maybe on Discord.

The Hermit Arcana is often associated with those who prefer solitude. They alone know how to process what is happening in life by withdrawing and ignoring the world and its noise and keeping to themselves in a quiet place. The hermit does not fear loneliness.
